Binaural Beat Frequency

The fascinating bridge between tones and rhythms can be quite a mind bender. Binaural Beats are essentially two tones at slightly different wavelengths which cause the ear to perceive a “beat”. Isochronic tones are tones which go off and repeat at certain regular intervals and are the same tone. The “beat” they make is an “actual” beat and that is how the brainwaves are entrained. There are also monaural beats which utilize the same technique as binaural beats, but since they are combined into a single audio channel, you do not need headphones to listen. Alpha Isochronic tones are specifically for the Alpha Brainwave State.
